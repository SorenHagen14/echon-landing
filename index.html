What we sell

Echon AI LLC provides software and implementation services for HVAC shops and other blue-collar service businesses:

AI Voice Receptionist

  • Answers your business line 24/7 — on the first ring, every time
  • Qualifies the caller: name, phone, address, service needed, urgency
  • Books appointments directly onto your schedule
  • Captures quote requests with the details you need to call back and close
  • Takes messages and flags calls that need your attention

Business dashboard

  • Every call recorded, transcribed, and summarized
  • Customer records, case tracking, and a schedule view of upcoming appointments
  • Call outcome analytics — see exactly where your calls go

Implementation & setup services

  • Dedicated business phone number provisioning
  • Call-forwarding setup from your existing business line
  • Configuration of your services, pricing rules, business hours, and calendar
  • Ongoing tuning of the receptionist to match how your shop actually operates
